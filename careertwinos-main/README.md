AICOS – AI Career Operating System

AICOS (AI Career Operating System) is an AI-powered platform designed to help students and professionals navigate their career journey through personalized insights, skill analysis, interview preparation, and career planning.

🚀 Features

Career Twin
Creates a personalized digital representation of a user's career profile based on skills, education, interests, and goals.

 Multi-Agent Hiring Panel
Simulates feedback from multiple AI hiring managers and recruiters to evaluate candidate readiness.

 Hiring Probability Simulator
Estimates the likelihood of success for a target role based on current skills and profile strength.

 Skill Gap Analysis
Identifies missing skills and knowledge areas required for desired career paths.

Personalized Learning Roadmap
Generates structured learning plans to bridge skill gaps and accelerate career growth.

 AI Mock Interviews
Provides realistic interview simulations and actionable feedback.

 Project Recommendation Engine
Suggests portfolio projects aligned with career goals and industry requirements.

 Job Readiness Score
Measures overall preparedness for job applications and interviews.

📌 Problem Statement

Many students and job seekers struggle to understand their current career position, identify skill gaps, and prepare effectively for job opportunities. AICOS solves this by providing personalized AI-powered career guidance and actionable recommendations.

🎯 Solution

AICOS combines multiple AI-powered career agents into a single platform that analyzes user profiles, recommends learning paths, conducts mock interviews, evaluates job readiness, and helps users make informed career decisions.
